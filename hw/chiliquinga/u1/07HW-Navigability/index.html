Welcome to the Grade Management System

This system is designed to efficiently manage grades, providing quick and secure access for students, teachers, and administrators.

Main Features

  • Real-time recording, updating, and consultation of grades.
  • Secure access control for different user profiles.
  • Generation of academic reports and performance statistics.
  • Intuitive interface accessible from any internet-connected device.

System Users

  • Administrators: Manage the system and configure settings.
  • Teachers: Record and view their students' grades.
  • Students: View their grades and academic performance.
  • Parents: Access grade reports and their children's performance statistics.

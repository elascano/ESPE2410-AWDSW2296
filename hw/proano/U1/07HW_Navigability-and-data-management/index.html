Welcome to the MADECOR Inventory and Sales System

This web application is designed to streamline inventory and sales management for MADECOR, a business specializing in educational toys, furniture, and more.

Purpose

The system provides an integrated solution for real-time inventory tracking, sales management, and decision-making support through detailed reports.

Features

  • View and manage the product catalog
  • Track sales and inventory updates in real time
  • Generate detailed sales and inventory reports
  • Role-based permissions for secure access
